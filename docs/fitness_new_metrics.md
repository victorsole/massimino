# European Fitness Industry 2024: Complete Market Rankings

The European fitness industry reached historic highs in 2024, with **€36 billion in total revenue** and **71.6 million gym memberships** across 64,550 health clubs—an 8.9% population penetration rate. Basic-Fit dominates as Europe's largest chain with 4.25 million members, while Myprotein leads supplements and Germany's Pamela Reif reigns as the continent's most-followed fitness influencer. This report provides comprehensive rankings for gyms, supplement companies, and fitness influencers across Europe and nine key national markets.

## Industry outlook for 2025 and beyond

EuropeActive targets **100 million members by 2030**, representing significant growth potential from the current 8.9% penetration rate versus 25% in the US. Key trends driving the industry include:

- **Consolidation accelerating**: Basic-Fit's Clever Fit acquisition and Vivagym's Altafit purchase signal continued M&A activity
- **Budget segment dominance**: HVLP (High-Value, Low-Price) operators capturing majority of new members
- **Digital integration**: Apps, connected equipment, and hybrid models becoming standard
- **Sports nutrition growth**: European market projected to reach €9-10 billion by 2030-2032
- **Clean-label supplements**: Plant-based proteins and transparency driving product innovation

The European fitness ecosystem has demonstrated remarkable post-pandemic recovery, with 2024 marking record membership and revenue figures that position the industry for continued expansion through the decade.
